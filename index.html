How to Play:

    Main Lotto Game

  • Winning numbers are displayed top center
  • Start the game by clicking "Click to play lotto"
  • Continue to draw by clicking "Draw Again"
  • You will receive $10.00 for every number matched to the winning lotto numbers

    • Power Play

  • You have an opportunity to bet your money in a powerplay round
  • If you match the powerplay number, your bet will be multiplied by 10.
  • If you do not match in powerplay, 1/4 of your bet * 10 goes to your opponent!
  • Winner has the highest bank value at the end of the game
  • Click "End Game" to tally up bank values and end the game.
